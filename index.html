About RGIPT JAIS

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT) is an autonomous institution established by the Government of India. The Institute is co-promoted as an energy domain-specific institute by six leading oil public sector units. The Institute has been accorded the status of "An Institution of National Importance" along the lines of the Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) under an Act of Parliament ("Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Act 2007").

Course Objective

This workshop provides an in-depth understanding of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) and the legal intricacies of patent filing. Participants will explore the significance of IPR in innovation, covering patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets. Through practical examination of patent filing procedures and legal frameworks, attendees will gain insights into protecting intellectual property assets, managing portfolios, and advancing their careers. Legal processes regarding infringement will also be addressed, ensuring a comprehensive grasp of the spectrum of IPR. About Workshop

This workshop comprehensive overview covers essential aspects of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), including copyrights, trademarks, patents, and trade secrets. The discussion starts with an exploration of these protections and then delves into the specifics of patent drafting and filing procedures. The journey continues to Licensing and Technology Transfer, examining how intellectual assets are shared. We also address IP Enforcement and Litigation, outlining legal avenues for protecting intellectual property. Open Innovation and Collaborative Research are explored, emphasizing the evolving landscape of collaborative efforts. The discourse shifts to IPR Management and emerging trends, shedding light on how intellectual assets are strategically handled. A crucial segment focuses on the Benefits of having a patent, emphasizing their industrial importance in driving innovation. In summary, this workshop provides a nuanced understanding of IPR, its management, and its impact on innovation, collaboration, and industrial development.
