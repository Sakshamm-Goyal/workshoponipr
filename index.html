About RGIPT JAIS

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT) is an autonomous institution established by the Government of India. The Institute is co-promoted as an energy domain-specific institute by six leading oil public sector units. The Institute has been accorded the status of "An Institution of National Importance" along the lines of the Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) under an Act of Parliament ("Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Act 2007").

Course Objective

This workshop provides an in-depth understanding of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) and the legal intricacies of patent filing. Participants will explore the significance of IPR in innovation, covering patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets. Through practical examination of patent filing procedures and legal frameworks, attendees will gain insights into protecting intellectual property assets, managing portfolios, and advancing their careers. Legal processes regarding infringement will also be addressed, ensuring a comprehensive grasp of the spectrum of IPR. These rights enable creators to benefit from their work, encourage innovation, and foster creativity by providing exclusive rights for a limited period, thereby promoting economic growth and societal progress.
